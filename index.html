FAQ

  • Who writes the reviews?

    All of the reviews on this website are written by a single person: Azaria Dunston. So take these reviews with a grain of salt as they are written from the subjective perspective of a single person.

  • How often are new reviews posted?

    Reviews are posted whenever Azaria has the time to. She is very busy with school and life.

  • Will you please write a review of [insert show/game]?

    Send me a message via our contact page and I'll see what I can do. However, I cannot make promises.

  • What is your criteria for rankings?

    For books, I rate based on the story progression, themes, writing style, emersion, and creativity.
    For dramas, the cinematography, actors' skill, themes, and writing
    For cartoons, I rate based on the animation quality and creativity, voice acting, writing
